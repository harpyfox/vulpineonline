this website is my personal hate letter to the modern web.

i do not want a website that moves. i do not want fades. i do not want pulldowns. i do not want scrolling animations. you would think as an animator i would enjoy these things, but the line between form and function has long been crossed. i enable 'reduced motion' on my devices if the option is enabled. that's how bad it is.

i just want a webpage that shows me what i want to see. that is my goal for this website: a portfolio that quickly and reliably shows you the shit you came here for.

technologies

most of the site is hand-written. Jinja2 is used to avoid copy-pasting the same <head> element with minor differences to every page.

the site is hosted with Cloudflare Workers. the documentation for their static site solution is a bit lacking and you can tell it's kind of a shoe-in to try and deprecate Cloudflare Pages. but it works well enough, and it's free. for now, at least.

javascript is minimal. the site functions without it.

github is used for version control. it deploys automatically when a push is made to the repository. isn't that nice.
