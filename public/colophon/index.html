colophon

this website is my personal hate letter to the modern web.

i do not want a website that moves. i do not want fades. i do not want pulldowns. i do not want scrolling animations. you would think as an animator i would enjoy these things, but the line between form and function has long been crossed.

i just want a webpage that shows me what i want to see. that is my goal for this website: a portfolio that quickly and reliably shows you the shit you came here for.
